Privacy Policy

Introduction

We want to keep your personal details safe and give you a positive experience using Workoutnote.

Personal information

We collect, store and use the following kinds of personal information:

  1. Information you provide when registering an account (including your email address)
  2. Your bodyweight as it changes over time
  3. Your performance in physical exercises

We only ask for details that will improve your experience and will not reveal personally identifiable information to third parties (name/email).

Facebook integration

When you connect to Facebook, we ask for personal details to improve your experience on Workoutnote. This includes your name, email and birthdate.

Whenever you login with Facebook, we request your list of friends. We will then connect you with other friends who use Workoutnote. We cannot see friends who do not use Workoutnote.

Non-personal information

We collect, store and use the following kinds of Non-personal information:

  1. Information about your computer and your visits to the website
  2. This includes your IP address, geographical location, and browser type and version

Use of cookies

We use cookies on every page to:

  1. Keep you logged in
  2. Remember your last lift (to help you enter your next one)

Google Analytics

Google provides us with usage statistics to help us improve Workoutnote. They use cookies to track how you interact with the website. This allows us to see the number of hits and other statistics.

Advertising

We use advertising to pay for our servers and fund development to improve Workoutnote.

We use a third-party to provide monetisation technologies for our site. You can review their privacy and cookie policy here.

Updates to this Policy

We reserve the right to update this document at any time.
